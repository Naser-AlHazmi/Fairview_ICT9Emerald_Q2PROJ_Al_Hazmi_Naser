Ful Recipe!



Ingredients:



  1. 2 (15-ounce) cans fava beans, drained and rinsed
  2. 1/4 cup olive oil
  3. 1 onion, chopped
  4. 4 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  6. 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  7. 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  8. 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  9. 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  10. Juice of 1 lemon
  11. Salt and black pepper to taste
  12. Optional garnishes:
  13. Hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  14. Chopped tomatoes
  15. Sliced cucumbers
  16. Pita bread


Instructions:



Heat the Oil:


Heat olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.

Sauté Aromatics:


Add chopped onion and garlic to the skillet and sauté until softened and fragrant.

Add Spices:


Stir in cumin, coriander, and red pepper flakes. Cook for another minute until fragrant.

Combine with Beans:


Add the drained and rinsed fava beans to the skillet.

Simmer:


Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the flavors have melded.

Finish and Serve:


Stir in chopped parsley and cilantro. Season with lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve hot with your choice of garnishes and pita bread.

Tips & Variations:



Spice Level: Adjust the amount of red pepper flakes to your preference.

Tahini:


For a richer flavor, stir in 2-3 tablespoons of tahini paste to the ful.

Tomatoes:


Add 1 (14.5-ounce) can of diced tomatoes (undrained) to the skillet along with the fava beans.

Smoked Paprika:


Add a pinch of smoked paprika for a smoky flavor.
