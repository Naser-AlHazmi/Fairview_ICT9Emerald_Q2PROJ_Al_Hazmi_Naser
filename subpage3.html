Shish Tawook Recipe!



Ingredients:



  1. 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes


    2. For the Marinade:


  2. 1 cup plain yogurt
  3. 1/4 cup lemon juice
  4. 1/4 cup olive oil
  5. 6 cloves garlic, minced
  6. 1 teaspoon paprika
  7. 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  8. 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  9. 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  10. 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  11. Salt to taste


Instructions:



Make the Marinade:


In a large bowl, whisk together yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, paprika, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, black pepper, and salt.

Marinate the Chicken:


Add the chicken cubes to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.

Prepare the Skewers:


If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent burning.

Thread the Chicken:


Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the skewers.

Grill or Broil:


Grill:
Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Grill the skewers for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.

Broil:
Preheat your broiler. Place the skewers on a baking sheet lined with foil. Broil for 5-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through.

Serve:


Remove the skewers from the heat source. Serve immediately with pita bread, hummus, tahini, and your favorite Middle Eastern salads.

Tips & Variations:



Spice it Up:


Add a pinch of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the marinade for extra heat.

Add Veggies:


Thread cherry tomatoes, onions, or bell peppers onto the skewers along with the chicken.

Yogurt Alternative:


If you're dairy-free, you can substitute the yogurt with plant-based yogurt or buttermilk.

Marinating Tips:


For even more flavor, marinate the chicken in a zip-top bag.
